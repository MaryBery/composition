5/23/22

I just finished my first year of my masters at Peabody!
This semester I composed a piano duet named Rage of the Priestess of Hecate based on the story of Madea and it was performed by Peabody alumni Choo Choo Hu and Michael Shephard. My classmates, Mike Begay and Nikko Masurace, and I also wrote a laptop ensemble piece named Blade Runner to tell a possible version of the apocalypse that may one day come to pass. Finally I have formed a laptop ensemble named Critical Error with my classmates Cameron Church, Sebastian Suarez-Solis, and Michael Mills, and we performed Cameron’s piece, Tile the Plane, at Virginia Tech’s Cube at the beginning of May.
